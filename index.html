About Us

Welcome to Matkotech

Matkotech is your trusted partner for innovative engineering and manufacturing solutions, delivering quality-driven services and technology solutions tailored to the evolving needs of modern industries.

We combine precision, innovation, and reliability to help businesses achieve operational excellence and sustainable growth through customer-centric execution and strong technical standards.

Our team works closely with clients across multiple industries to provide dependable support, efficient execution, and scalable manufacturing capabilities.

Led by Director Hardik Patel, Matkotech is driven by a vision to deliver world-class engineering solutions with integrity, technical depth, and a commitment to long-term client success.
